Partners

Acknowledging and thanking Slug Gaming's partners.

Tespa

As the leader in collegiate esports both on campus and on the competitive stage, Tespa promotes gaming as a platform for healthy competition and a catalyst for building communities. Tespa offers year-round tournaments, event support, and other membership benefits for hundreds of college gaming clubs across the country. We are proud to represent them as the Tespa chapter at UC Santa Cruz.


Ubisoft

Ubisoft is a leading video game developer, dedicated to creating memorable and exciting gaming experiences for all players. Their commitment to quality is reflected in their well-received, triple-A titles like the Assassin’s Creed, Prince of Persia, and Far Cry franchises.


Pixel My Heart

A rising game developer stemming from our very own UC Santa Cruz itself, Pixel My Heart has recently released Writer’s Block, a “side-scrolling horror narrative” that’s as creepy as it is innovative. Check out their game and stay up to date with news and updates here.


Patriot Viper Gaming

A maker of top-of-the-line, high performance memory, SSD, and flash storage solutions, Viper Gaming has since expanded to creating gaming keyboards, headsets, and mice. We are proud to use Viper’s products, which have become synonymous with performance and reliability for gamers worldwide.


Twitch

As the world’s top platform for live streaming and sharing content, Twitch attracts millions of viewers each day to watch their favorite content creators. With an easy-to-use interface and a massive audience, anyone can stream on Twitch and become a content creator. We invite you to join the millions of people already sharing and viewing content on the largest social video platform today.
