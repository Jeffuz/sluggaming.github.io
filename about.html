About Slug Gaming

Who we are

UCSC's Slug Gaming goal is to bring together students who play al kinds of games. Our goal is to build a student community surrounding the game which supports game discussion, casual/competitive play, and participation in club events.

The Slug Gaming club assists competitively-minded players with finding teammates and preparing for game tournaments hosted by Tespa, Collegiate StarLeague, and various other organizations.

Mission Statement

To establish and support a friendly, welcoming community for both casual and competitive gamers at UC Santa Cruz.

Administration Officers
