About Slug Gaming

Who we are

We are UC Santa Cruz's gaming club. We meet bi-weekly and host a variety of other gaming events throughout hte school year.

Mission Statement

To establish and support a friendly, welcoming community for both casual and competitive gamers at UC Santa Cruz.
