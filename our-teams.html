HERE ARE THE VARIOUS GAMES WE PLAY IN SLUG GAMING!
CALL OF DUTY
Call of Duty is a first-person shooter video game franchise published by Activision. The games in the Call of Duty series are known for their fast-paced, intense gameplay and realistic military themes. Players assume the role of soldiers in various military conflicts, and the games often feature a single-player campaign mode that follows a story, as well as multiplayer modes that allow players to compete against each other online. The Call of Duty series has released many installments, each set in a different time period and featuring its own unique story, gameplay mechanics, and set of characters. Some of the most popular installments in the series include Call of Duty: Modern Warfare, Call of Duty: Black Ops, and Call of Duty: WWII. Overall, the Call of Duty series is known for its immersive gameplay, high-quality graphics, and strong competitive multiplayer components. It is a popular choice among first-person shooter fans and competitive gamers.
HEARTHSTONE
Hearthstone is an online card game game about optimizing small decisions, probability, and statistics all to reduce your opponent's health to 0. This year, the team hopes to grow the UCSC Hearthstone community to show that even schools without a varsity esports program still pose a threat in the competitive scene.
LEAGUE OF LEGENDS
League of Legends is a free-to-play MOBA game that pits two teams of powerful champions to battle head-to-head across multiple battlefields. Since its release in 2009, it has grown to be the most played PC game with the largest competitive scene worldwide. UCSC's League of Legends competitive team has an extensive history in the collegiate tournament scene. Notable achievements includes: a top 16 finish in a western conference playoff run, 3rd place in ACEL (WEC), and Fanime tournament champions. The team is looking forward to more championship runs in the future!
OVERWATCH
Overwatch is a competitive team-based first person shooter game. Published by Blizzard Entertainment Overwatch in 2016, the made top 20 nationally & placed second in Fanime Overwatch tournament. This year the Division 1 team strives to make a mark in the school's esports competitive scene by competing in the Tespa College Championship Series. We are aiming to be top 8 on the charts. The Overwatch division embraces an all-inclusive environment where gamers can discover, discuss, learn, share tips, and play Overwatch with fellow colleagues! In addition, we offer opportunities for people to experience the social and competitive aspects of Overwatch. Whether you play casually or competitively, this organization welcomes all stuudents to join!
RAINBOW SIX SIEGE
Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege is a tactical first-person shooter video game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and published by Ubisoft. It was released in 2015 for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One. In the game, players assume the roles of operators belonging to different counter-terrorist units, and engage in intense, destructible environments. The game features an asymmetrical structure, where each player on a team has a unique role with their own special abilities. One team takes on the role of attackers, while the other team plays as defenders. The objective for the attackers is to plant a bomb or rescue a hostage, while the defenders must prevent this from happening. Rainbow Six Siege has received critical acclaim for its destructible environments, tactical gameplay, and intense multiplayer matches. It has a strong esports scene, with multiple professional leagues and tournaments held around the world.
ROCKET LEAGUE
Rocket League combines soccer with driving in a physics-based multiplayer game which satisfies cravings for fast-paced gamepaly and montage-worthy moments for players willing to face mount champion. The 2018/2019 school year marks the beginning of Slug Gaming's official support of Rocket League. We plan on getting the game the recognition it deserves and drafting a competitive team capable of taking on the powerhouses of Collegiate Rocket League.
SPLATOON
Splatoon is a third-person shooter video game developed and published by Nintendo for the Wii U. In Splatoon, players control characters called Inklings, who are able to transform between humanoid and squid forms. The objective of the game is to cover the most territory with ink, using weapons that shoot streams of colored ink. The game features a variety of multiplayer modes, including "Turf War", in which two teams compete to cover the most territory with ink, and "Splat Zones", in which players compete to control specific areas of the map. Splatoon also features a single-player campaign mode, in which players must defeat AI-controlled enemies. The game has a unique and colorful art style, and its mechanics encourage players to think strategically and work as a team. Splatoon was well-received upon release and has developed a dedicated player base. It has become a popular choice for players looking for a unique and fun multiplayer experience.
VALORANT
VALORANT is a free-to-play first-person shooter video game developed and published by Riot Games. It was released in 2020 for Microsoft Windows. In the game, players assume the roles of agents, each with their own unique abilities and playstyles. The game features a 5v5 objective-based mode called "Spike Rush", in which teams compete to plant and defend a bomb, as well as a more traditional "Escalation" mode in which players compete in rounds to eliminate the enemy team or complete objectives. VALORANT is known for its tactical gameplay and character-based abilities, which require players to think strategically and work as a team to succeed. The game has a strong esports scene, with professional leagues and tournaments being held around the world. Overall, VALORANT is a popular choice among first-person shooter fans and competitive gamers, and it has gained a dedicated player base since its release.
